POLICIES

FINANCING:

  • We accepted credit cards for any purchase five dollars or more.
  • Acceptable credit cars include VISA, MasterCard, Discover, & American Express
  • We do not accept checks.
  • We only accept US dollars.
  • DHS giftcards are available to be purchased at any of our three locations and never expire.

DELIVERIES:

  • All orders must be paid in full before delivery.
  • There is a flat fee of $3.99 for all deliveries.
  • For deliveries of live animals please ensure the house is prepared.

RETURNS:

  • All unopened food can be returned with a receipt.
  • All unused tanks and toys can be returned within 60 days of purchase.
  • Without a receipt, items can only be exchanged for store credit.
  • In the first twenty days of ownership an animal can be returned or refunded for half the price.
  • The above rule includes injured and deceased animals.


For more information please contact our HR Representatives at 1(603) 123-0099 ext 4554.
